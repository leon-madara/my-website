# Requirements Document

## Introduction

A modern, responsive landing page redesign featuring geometric flag elements, dotted background patterns, and personal branding for a Full Stack AI Developer. The design emphasizes clean aesthetics with strategic use of SVG graphics and subtle background textures.

## Glossary

- **Landing_Page**: The main entry point webpage that introduces the developer and their services
- **Flag_Elements**: SVG graphics (hanginLFT.svg and Left-flag.svg) that provide visual interest and branding
- **Dot_Pattern**: A subtle tiled background pattern of small dots at 3% opacity
- **Responsive_Layout**: A design that adapts to different screen sizes while maintaining visual hierarchy
- **SVG_Graphics**: Scalable vector graphics for crisp display at all resolutions

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to see a visually striking landing page that immediately communicates the developer's expertise, so that I can quickly understand their capabilities and services.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL display at a 2560x1440 aspect ratio for desktop viewing
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL feature the name "Leon Madara" prominently in the hero section
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL display "Full Stack AI Developer" as the primary role descriptor
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL incorporate the Flag_Elements as visual design components
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL apply a Dot_Pattern background at 3% opacity across the entire page

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want the page to load quickly and display correctly across different devices, so that I can access the content regardless of my device or connection speed.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL be responsive and adapt to mobile, tablet, and desktop screen sizes
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL optimize SVG_Graphics for fast loading without quality loss
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL maintain visual hierarchy and readability on all device sizes
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL use efficient CSS and HTML structure for optimal performance

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to easily navigate and interact with the page elements, so that I can find relevant information and contact details efficiently.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL include clear navigation elements or call-to-action buttons
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL provide accessible color contrast ratios for all text elements
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL implement hover states and interactive feedback for clickable elements
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL include social media or contact links in an easily discoverable location

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As the developer, I want the page to accurately represent my brand and technical expertise, so that potential clients and collaborators can assess my capabilities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL incorporate the existing Flag_Elements (hanginLFT.svg and Left-flag.svg) in their proper layered arrangement
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL use a modern color scheme that complements the flag graphics
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL maintain consistent typography and spacing throughout the design
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL showcase technical skills through clean, professional code implementation
