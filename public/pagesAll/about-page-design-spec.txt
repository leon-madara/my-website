## Animations

### Parallax Scroll Effects
- **Green Section**: Expands 0 to 20vw
- **Hero Container**: Scales and translates
- **Hero Image**: Parallax movement
- **About Text**: Fade and slide

### Section Entrance Animations
- **Title**: Fade in from back
- **Body**: Slide up from below
- **Cards**: Staggered fade-in

### Scroll-Triggered Animations
- **Library**: GSAP 3.12.5 + ScrollTrigger
- **Timing**: Triggered at specific scroll positions
- **Easing**: Smooth cubic-bezier curves

## Scroll Behavior

### Parallax Implementation
- **JavaScript**: `js/about-parallax.js`
- **Library**: GSAP ScrollTrigger
- **Behavior**:
  - Hero section animates during initial scroll
  - Content sections fade in as they enter viewport
  - Smooth, continuous scrolling

### Scroll Indicators
- **Green Bar**: Visual progress indicator
- **Smooth Scroll**: Native smooth scroll behavior

---

## Responsive Behavior

### Breakpoints
- **Mobile** (`< 768px`):
  - Single column layout
  - Reduced parallax effects
  - Simplified animations

- **Tablet** (`768px - 1024px`):
  - 2-column grids
  - Moderate parallax

- **Desktop** (`> 1024px`):
  - Full 3-column grids
  - Full parallax effects

### Mobile Optimizations
- Parallax disabled or simplified
- Grid columns reduce to 1-2
- Font sizes scale down
- Reduced animation complexity

## Notes for Recreation

1. **Parallax Timing**: Green section expansion should sync with hero scaling
2. **Scroll Smoothness**: Use GSAP ScrollTrigger for buttery smooth animations
3. **Section Spacing**: Generous whitespace between sections
4. **Grid Responsiveness**: Grids should collapse gracefully on mobile
5. **Timeline Design**: Vertical line should connect all timeline markers
6. **Card Consistency**: All cards share similar padding, border-radius, shadow
7. **Emoji Icons**: Use native emoji for cross-platform consistency
8. **Tag Styling**: Pill-shaped with subtle hover effects
9. **Color Accents**: Use Kenyan gradient sparingly for emphasis
10. **Scroll Performance**: Optimize for 60fps on all devices
