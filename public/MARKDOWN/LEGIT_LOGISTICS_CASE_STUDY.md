# Legit Logistics: Delivery Operations Platform

## Case Study by Leon Madara, Full Stack Developer

---

## Executive Summary

**Legit Logistics** is an owner-operated delivery operations platform that connects service marketing, quote review, dispatch management, public tracking, and driver proof-of-delivery in one experience. Built with React, TypeScript, Supabase, and Tailwind CSS, the product pairs a polished logistics brand with practical operational tooling for same-day and final-mile delivery work.

### Key Outcomes
- **15,000+ deliveries completed** reflected in the live product story
- **99.2% on-time rate** highlighted as a core trust metric
- **500+ business clients** supported by the operation
- **Under 2 minutes** to create a new delivery job in the admin dashboard
- **Public tracking + driver proof** integrated into the same workflow

---

## Product Story

Legit Logistics needed more than a simple marketing site. The business required a connected product that could explain services clearly, collect and negotiate quotes, turn approved work into dispatchable jobs, share trustworthy tracking links, and close deliveries with proof.

The resulting platform spans:
- service-led landing and marketing pages
- quote review, accept, decline, and counter-offer flows
- public tracking and order lookup routes
- an admin dashboard for delivery creation and management
- mobile-friendly driver status, photo, and signature workflows

---

## Why It Works

A major design decision was to make tracking **trust-first**. Instead of exposing tracking too early, the product only reveals customer tracking after a delivery is actually confirmed. That small workflow choice makes the experience feel more honest and more operationally grounded.

The platform also reflects the needs of an owner-operated business: responsive communication, service clarity, and a dashboard that keeps dispatch actions visible without unnecessary complexity.

---

## Technical Highlights

- **Frontend:** React 18, TypeScript, Vite
- **UI System:** Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui, Framer Motion
- **Platform:** Supabase for auth, data, storage, and subscriptions
- **Forms and State:** React Hook Form, Zod, TanStack Query

The architecture blends public conversion pages with real operations tooling, making the project a strong example of software that supports both growth and execution.

---

## Operational Impact

Legit Logistics demonstrates how thoughtful frontend and product engineering can improve both customer trust and dispatch efficiency. Quote handling, delivery creation, tracking visibility, and proof collection now reinforce each other instead of living in disconnected tools.

That makes this project a strong portfolio case study for product thinking, workflow design, and full-stack implementation quality.
